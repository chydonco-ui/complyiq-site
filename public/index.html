The Problem

Fragmented. Fast‑Moving. High‑Risk.

Nigerian institutions juggle overlapping mandates from CBN, SEC, NAICOM, NDIC, NFIU, NCC, FIRS and state‑level bodies. Updates are frequent, formats inconsistent, and enforcement is tightening — leaving compliance teams constantly reacting instead of staying ahead.

  • Manual monitoring of circulars, guidelines, directives and press releases.
  • Slow, spreadsheet‑driven analysis that delays implementation and increases risk.
  • High likelihood of missed obligations, late responses and regulatory sanctions.
  • Compliance teams stuck firefighting instead of driving strategic risk management.
Our Solution

A Single Source of Regulatory Truth — Powered by Automated Intelligence

ComplyIQ continuously monitors Nigeria’s regulatory ecosystem, interprets every update, extracts obligations, and operationalises change across your organisation — with speed, accuracy and audit‑ready traceability.

  • Real‑time regulatory ingestion — every circular, guideline, directive and update captured the moment it’s released.
  • AI‑driven interpretation — obligations, deadlines, thresholds and penalties extracted with precision.
  • Automated change workflows — assign owners, track progress, capture evidence and close actions.
  • Executive‑ready dashboards — instant visibility for CROs, COOs, legal teams and boards.
